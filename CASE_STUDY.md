# Case Study: Lead Processing Automation

## Client Need
Automate lead intake from a Google Form and push leads to multiple systems instantly.

## Previous Workflow
- Manual data entry into spreadsheets
- Trello tasks created by hand
- Team notifications delayed or missed
- Calendar reminders often forgotten
- Manual CRM updates

## Problems Identified
- Slow lead response times
- High risk of missed tasks
- Time-consuming manual work

## Implemented Solution
An end-to-end automation built in n8n:
- Triggered on new Google Form submission
- Data normalized and validated
- Trello task created with all details
- Telegram notification sent to team
- Google Calendar reminder added
- Contact automatically created in Agile CRM

## Results
- 100% of leads processed automatically
- Response time reduced from hours to seconds
- No missed tasks or follow-ups
- Workflow fully scalable
- Team can focus on actual sales work

## Business Impact
The workflow removed manual operations, reduced errors, and accelerated lead response, dramatically improving onboarding and team productivity.
